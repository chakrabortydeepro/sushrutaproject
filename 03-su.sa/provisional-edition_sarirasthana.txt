                    <note>The text of this adhyāya is a duplicate of NAK 5-333, waiting to

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.1"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.1</label>
                         athātaḥ sarvvabhūtacintāṃ śārīraṃ vyākhyāsyāmaḥ ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.2"/>


                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.3"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.3</label>
                         sarvvabhūtānāṅ kāraṇam akāraṇaṃ satvarajastamolakṣaṇam aṣṭarūpam
                        akhilasyāsya jagataḥ sambhavahetur avyaktannāma || tad ekam
                        anekeṣāṃ kṣetrajñānām adhiṣṭhānāṃ | samudra ivodakaujasām
                        bhāvānāṃ</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.4"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.4</label>
                         tasmād avyaktān mahān utpadyate talliṅga eva | talliṅgāc ca
                        mahatas tallakṣaṇa evāhaṅkāra utpadyate | sa trividho vaikārikas
                        taijaso bhūtādir iti | tatra vaikārikād ahaṃkārāt tallakṣaṇāny
                        evaikādaśendriyāṇy utpadyante || tad yathā | śrotraṃ tvak cakṣur
                        jjihvā ghrāṇo vāg ghastopasthapāyupādamanānsīti | tatra pūrvvāṇi
                        pañca vuddhīndriyāṇi | itarāṇi pañca karmmendriyāṇi | ubhayātmakam
                        manaḥ | bhūtādes tallakṣaṇāny eva pañcatanmātrāṇy utpadyante ||
                        tad yathā || śabdatanmātraṃ | sparśatanmātraṃ | rūpatanmātraṃ |
                        rasatanmātraṃ | gandhatanmātram iti | tebhyo bhūtāni
                        vyomānilānalajalor vvyastebhyas tadviśeṣāḥ
                        śabdasparśarūparasagandhāḥ | evam etāni caturvviṃśati tatvāni
                        vyākhyātāni bhavanti |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.5"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.5</label>
                         tatra buddhīndriyāṇāṃ śabdādayo viṣayāḥ | karmmendriyāṇāṃ
                        yathāsaṃkhyāvacanādān ānandavisarggaviharaṇāni |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.6"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.6</label>
                         avyaktam mahān ahaṃkāraḥ pañcatanmātrāṇi cety aṣṭau prakṛtayaḥ
                        śeṣāḥ ṣoḍaśavikārāḥ</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.7"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.7</label>
                         svaṃ svaṃ svaiṣāṃ viṣaye vibhutvaṃ | svayam adhyātmam adhidevan
                        tu | atha vuddher vvrahyā | ahaṃkārasyeśvaraḥ | manasaś candramāḥ
                        | diśaḥ śrotrasya | tvaco vāyuḥ | sūryaś cakṣuṣaḥ | rasanā āpaḥ |
                        pṛthivī ghrāṇasya | vāco 'gniḥ | hastayor indra | pādayor vviṣṇuḥ
                        | pāyor mmitraḥ | prajāpatir upasthasyeti | </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.8"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.8</label>
                         tatra sarvva evācetanaḥ | eṣa varggaḥ | puruṣaś ca
                        pañcaviṃśatitamaś cetayitā | sa tu karaṇakāryasaṃprayuktaḥ saty
                        apy acetanye pradhānasya puruṣakaivalyārthān pravṛttim upadiśanti
                        | kṣīrādīm̐ś cātra hetūr udāharanti ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.9"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.9</label>
                         ata ūrdhvam prakṛtipuruṣayoḥ sādharmmyavaidharmmye vyākhyāsyāmaḥ
                        || ubhāv apy anādī ubhāv apy anantau | ubhāv apy anityau | ubhāv
                        apy aparau ubhāv apy aliṅgau ubhau ca sarvvagatāv iti || ekā tu
                        prakṛtir acetanā triguṇā vījadharmmiṇī prasavadharmminī
                        madhyadharmmiṇī ceti | vahavas tu puruṣāś cetanāv antaḥ | aguṇā
                        avījadharmmiṇo prasavadharmmiṇo madhyasthadharmmiṇaś ceti |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.10"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.10</label>
                         tatra kāraṇānurūpaṃ kāryam iti kṛtvā sarvva evaite ca viśeṣāḥ |
                        satvarajastamomayā bhavanti | tad añcanatvāt tanmayatvāc ca
                        tadguṇe eva puruṣo bhavatīty eke bhāṣante</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.11"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.11</label>
                         vaidyake tu ||
                        <caesura/>
                         svabhāvo niyatiḥ kālaḥ pariṇāmas tatheśvaraḥ |
                        <caesura/>
                         yadṛcchati ca manyante prakṛtim pṛthudarśiṇaḥ ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.12"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.12</label>
                         tato jātāni bhūtāni tadguṇāny eva nirddiśet |
                        <caesura/>
                         tebhyas tallakṣaṇaḥ kṛtsno bhūtagrāmaḥ prajāyate ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.13"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.13</label>
                         tasyopayogo bhihitaś cikitsām prati sarvvadā |
                        <caesura/>
                         bhūtebhyo 'pi paran tasmān nāsti cintā cikitsite ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.14"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.14</label>
                         yato 'bhihitaṃ tat sambhavadravyasamūho bhūtādir ukta iti |
                        bhautikāni cendriyāṇy āyurvvede varṇṇyante tathaivendriyārthāḥ
                        ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.15"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.15</label>
                         bhavati cātra || indriyair indriyārthaṃ tu svaṃ svaṃ gṛhṇāti
                        mānavaḥ |
                        <caesura/>
                         niyataṃ tulyayonitvān nāmyenānyam iti sthitiḥ ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.16"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.16</label>
                         na cāyurvvedaśāstreṣūpadiśyante || sarvvagatākṣetrajñanityāś ca
                        asarvvagateṣu ca kṣetrajñeṣv anityeṣu ca puruṣākhyāpakān hetūn
                        udāharanti | āyurvvedasiddhāntād asarvvagatā kṣetrajñā nityāś ca |
                        tiryagyonimānuṣadeveṣu ca sañcaranti | dharmmādharmmanimittaṃ tu
                        etenumānagrāhyāś carāḥ paramaśūkṣmāś cetanāvantaḥ śāśvatāḥ
                        śukrārttavayoḥ sannipāteṣv abhivyajyante | yato 'bhihitam yañ ca
                        mahābhūtaḥ śarīrasamavāyaḥ puruṣa iti | sa khalv eṣa karmmapuruṣaś
                        cikitsāyām adhikṛtaḥ |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.17"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.17</label>
                         tasya khalu sukhaduḥkhecchādveṣaprayatnaḥ prāṇāpānau vuddhir
                        mmanaḥ saṃkalpo vicāraṇānusmṛtijñānam adhyavasāyo viṣayopalabdhiś
                        ca guṇāḥ |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.18"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.18</label>
                         sātvikās tu ānṛśaṃsyaṃ samvibhāgarucitā titikṣā satyan dharmma
                        āstikyaṃ jñānaṃ vuddhir mmedhā smṛtir anabhiṣvaṅgatā ca || rājasās
                        tu duḥkhavahulatāghradānaṃ duḥśīlatvam akāruṇyam ānṛtikatvam
                        ahaṃkāro dambho māno harṣaḥ kāmakrodhaś ceti || tāmasās tu
                        viṣāditvaṃ nāstikyam adharmmaśīlatā vuddhinirodho 'jñānaṃ
                        durmmedhastvam akarmmaśīlatā nidrālutvañ ceti ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.19"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.19</label>
                         antarikṣās tu śabdaśabdendriyaṃ sarvvacchidrasamūho viviktatā ca
                        | vāyavyās tu sparśaḥ sparśendriyaṃ sarvvaceṣṭāsamūhaḥ
                        sarvvaśarīspandaranaṃ laghutā ca | taijasās tu rūpaṃ rūpendriyaṃ
                        varṇṇasantāpo bhrājiṣṇutā paktir amarṣas taikṣṇyañ ca | āpyās tu
                        raso ca rasendriyaṃ sarvvadravasamūho gurutā śaityaṃ sneho retaś
                        ca | pārthivās tu gandho gandhendriyaṃ sarvvamūrttisamūho gurutā
                        ca |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.20"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.20</label>
                         tatra satvavahulam ākāśaṃ | rajovahulo vāyuḥ | satvarajovahulo
                        'gniḥ | satvatamovahula āpaḥ | tamovahulā pṛthivī ||</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.21"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.21</label>
                         bhavati cātra ||
                        <caesura/>
                         anyonyāni praviṣṭāni sarvvāṇy etāni nirddiśet |
                        <caesura/>
                         sve sve dravyeṣu sarvveṣāṃ vyaktalakṣaṇam iṣyate ||</l>


                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.22"><label>1931 ed. 3.1.22</label>
                         aṣṭau prakṛtayaḥ proktā vikārāś caiva ṣoḍaśa |
                        <caesura/>
                         kṣetrajñāś ca samāsena svatantraparatantrata iti ||</l>

                    <p ana="3.1.trailer">śārīrasya prathamo 'dhyāyaḥ || </p>
