====== Suśruta Project manuscript transcriptions of MS London IOLR Taylor 1842 (Eggeling 2646). ======


This is the repository for the manuscript transcriptions  of the Suśrutapāṭhaśuddhi of Candraṭa from MS IOLR Taylor 1842 (Eggeling 2646) created as part of the [[http://sushrutaproject.org|sushrutaproject.org]], q.v. for the full details.

This repository is currently maintained by Dominik Wujastyk.

===== Reading and collating manuscript transcriptions =====

The manuscript collations and provisional edition are handled by [[https://github.com/chchch/upama|Saktumiva and Upama]].

  * [[https://206.12.96.209/dokuwiki/doku.php?id=wiki%3Asusrutapathasuddhi%3Asusrutapathasuddhi-01|Sūtrasthāna]]
  * [[https://206.12.96.209/dokuwiki/doku.php?id=wiki%3Asusrutapathasuddhi%3Asusrutapathasuddhi-02|Nidānasthāna]]
  * [[https://206.12.96.209/dokuwiki/doku.php?id=wiki%3Asusrutapathasuddhi%3Asusrutapathasuddhi-03|Śārīrasthāna]]
  * [[https://206.12.96.209/dokuwiki/doku.php?id=wiki%3Asusrutapathasuddhi%3Asusrutapathasuddhi-06|Uttaratantra]]


Here are [[https://saktumiva.org/wiki/users|further instructions for readers]].
